Refund Policy

This Refund Policy governs refunds related to plot booking under the Khatu Shyam Ji Residential Scheme.

Booking Amount Refund
  • If an applicant’s name does not appear in the lucky draw allotment list, the ₹2,000 booking amount will be fully refunded.
  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment source within a reasonable time.
Non-Refundable Cases
  • If the applicant is selected in the lucky draw but fails to complete registry within the stipulated time, the booking amount may be forfeited.
  • No refund will be applicable after successful plot allotment and registry initiation.
Refund Processing
  • Refunds are subject to verification of application and payment details.
  • The company is not responsible for delays caused by banking or payment gateway issues.
Contact Information

For booking, refund, or policy-related queries, please contact the official numbers mentioned in the scheme pamphlet.
